OOTD Launch

short launch commercial for OOTD — shot on digital and graded to feel like film.

About

a playful, summer picnic built around the OOTD notification. the goal was to feel like a memory: soft light, warm tones, and subtle motion.

Role

  • direction, cinematography, and edit — PJ Kim

Details

  • Format: 4K digital, graded to emulate color film
  • Camera: Fujifilm XS-20, handheld, natural light with reflectors
  • Tools: Premiere Pro, After Effects (text + notification overlays)
