Lost

a short film about finding fullfilment

About

a small reflection piece about losing myself in the constant chase for status — recruiting, social media, networking — and realizing how little of it was rooted in real connection. the film sits with that tension: feeling lost, recognizing the emptiness, and slowly remembering what actually fulfills me. at its core, it’s about coming back to the things that bring people together, and choosing to create from a place of honesty rather than expectation.

Role

  • direction, cinematography, and edit — PJ Kim

Details

  • Format: 4K digital, desaturated with soft grain for a muted look
  • Camera: Fujifilm XS-20 + primes, natural lighting
  • Tools: Premiere Pro (cut + grade)
  • Music: Moon by Daniel Caesar
