DECEMBER

A performance-driven music video for Lizette’s debut single.

December 2025

About

Lizette Mina reached out to me via instagram on 9/5/25 looking to for help with her music video. After listening to her song, I gladly said yes, and we started planning for the video. After a few meetings to storyboard shots and plan logistics, we reached out to Meekel T-Riney to act in the video. We filmed in December to match the winter landscape described in the music.

Role

  • Director / Cinematography / Edit — PJ Kim
